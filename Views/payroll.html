Payroll Computation

Payroll Generation > Payroll Computation

1. Only active employee(s) Payroll can be generate.
2. Only Current month and Year Payroll can be generate.
3. Deduction of Leave days is not included in the Net Salary.
4. The bonus amount can be null.
5. If bonus amount is entered then it is added to the net salary at the last
6. Gross Salary is equal to the sum of Extras and Basic Salary.
7. Net Salary is calculated by minus the Substractions and Gross Salary.
